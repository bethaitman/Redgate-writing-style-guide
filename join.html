Become a member

Membership criteria

To be a Make Do and Mend member, you must live in Cambridgeshire and have experience of mental health distress.

How to join

  1. Fill out the Membership Application Form.
  2. Send us the form at makedoandmendmail@gmail.com. We want to be as inclusive as possible, so if you have any particular requirements that'll let you take part in a workshop, mention it in the email.
  3. We'll get in touch and agree a support contract with you (in case you need medical help during a workshop).

That's it! You'll then be able to attend workshops, volunteer your own skills, and benefit from our information and signposting services.

The information you send us will be handled in strictest confidence. It won't be shared with anyone else without your permission.
