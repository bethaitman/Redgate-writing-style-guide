Workshops

At Make, Do and Mend, our members can take part in workshops that match their interests, run by volunteers who share their time and skills. Our workshops are sustainable, low-cost, and empowering. They nurture self-esteem, personality and character using a strengths-based approach.

We believe that sharing talents with others builds better communities, and strengthens social networks for people with experience of mental health distress. In our workshops, we'll help you master new skills in a supportive, relaxed environment.
