Become a Friend

Becoming a Friend of Make, Do and Mend means we'll keep you up-to-date through our Newsletter. You'll also get one free pass to attend a workshop of your choice, so you can experience first-hand how our workshops, with your support, help our members. We hope you might even be inspired to come and share your own strengths and talents at a future workshop.

Friend membership costs £12 per year.
