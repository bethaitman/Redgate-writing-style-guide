Make, Do and Mend offers skills workshops, peer support, volunteering opportunities and information on local mental health resources.

We're a not-for-profit organisation in Cambridge offering support to people who have experience of mental health distress. We're funded and supported by Cambridge City Council and ESF Community Grants, co-financed by the Skills Funding Agency and European Union, and co-ordinated by TCHC.
