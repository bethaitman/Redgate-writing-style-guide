Study Guatemala provides high quality education to the most marginalized children in Guatemala City. We train and support children in achieving educational qualifications to help improve their quality of life, and the health and wellbeing of their families and communities.

Our goal, at its simplest, is to help those in at risk get access to free education, and ultimately to improve their life chances.
